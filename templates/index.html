About NEURAL UPLOAD

Neural Upload is a open source project to identify images trough a neural network. The project is based on Python. 

The Neural network is trained to identify:

  • Cars
  • Dogs
  • Cats
  • Frogs
  • Airplanes
  • Horses
  • Ships
  • Deers
  • Birds
  • Trucks

I have plans to increase the categories and subjects in the future.
